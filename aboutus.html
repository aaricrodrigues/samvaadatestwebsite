About Samvaada

Samvaada, the official Social Media and Documentation Club of NMAMIT has been a testament to understanding and embracing content in and around the campus. With a shared commitment to excellence, our dedicated team works to shape the digital landscape of our institution through captivating content over various platforms. At the core of our mission lies a singular objective: to curate and deliver great digital experiences that resonate with our college community and beyond. From engaging videos that showcase campus life to fun-filled shorts, we leverage the power of storytelling to inform, inspire, and connect.
